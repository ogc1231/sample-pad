Instructions

  • 01

    What is a Sample Pad

    Play any sound you want, perfect for when you don't have the physical instrument, the space or the means to make the required sound available.

  • 02

    How to use

    • The volume slider controls the output volume of the audio when played.

    • The checkbox changes the bank of audio available when engaged and switches back to the orginal audio file bank when disengaged.

    • To play an audio file press the pad button with you mouse or finger on touchscreens.

    • You can also play the audio files by pressing the corresponding keyboard key named on your keyboard.
